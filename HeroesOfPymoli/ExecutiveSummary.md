# Heroes Of Pymoli Data Analysis

## Executive Summary





There were 780 purchasings of items that enhance the player experience of the game. The purchasing was 179 items with an average price of 3.05. Also, these items purchased generated 2,379.77 revenue for the company.

The players who made this revenue were 576 (84.03% males and 14.06% females). Also, the age range is complete with children less than ten years old to adults, but the players who bought more were people in their twenties (20-24) with 44.49% of the total the players, following by teens between 15 and 19 with 18.58%, and third people in their late twenties 25 to 29 with 13.37%.  

The players bought a least 1 item, although a few players whose purchase are more than three items; only one player bought five articles. On the other hand, for the players who have various objects to choose from, the most purchased item was “Final Critic” with only 13 purchases. The second “Oathbreaker, Last Hope of the Breaking Storm” with 12 items purchased. Also, these were the most profitable.
